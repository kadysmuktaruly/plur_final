Frequently asked

How is this different from Khan Academy or other SAT prep?+
Unlike static question banks, PLUR Tutor generates fresh problems every time using AI — so you never run out of practice material and never memorize answers. Each problem is unique and calibrated to your chosen difficulty level.
What math topics does it cover?+
PLUR Tutor focuses on SAT algebra: linear equations, systems of equations, quadratics, polynomial manipulation, inequalities, word problems, and function composition — exactly what appears on the SAT Math section.
How does the difficulty system work?+
Easy covers basic algebra (6th–8th grade level). Medium matches the SAT Math section difficulty. Hard includes the toughest SAT-level problems involving polynomial manipulation and complex function composition.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?+
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period — no questions asked.
Do I need to create an account?+
Yes — a free account tracks your progress, saves history, and puts you on the leaderboard. Sign up with email or Google in seconds. No credit card needed.
How long until I see score improvements?+
Most students see measurable improvement within 3–4 weeks of daily practice (20–30 min/day). Consistent Hard-level practice typically produces the biggest score jumps.
